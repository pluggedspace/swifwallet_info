Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Home

1. What is Swif?

Swif is a cross-border digital wallet built on the Stellar network that allows users to send, receive, and manage money globally with ease. It focuses on low fees, fast transactions, and user-friendly features, supporting multiple payment methods.

2. How can I create a Swif account?

To create a Swif account, simply download the app, provide your personal details, and complete the verification process. After verifying your email and identity, you’ll be able to use all Swif features.

3. How do I add funds to my Swif wallet?

You can add funds by linking your bank account, debit/credit card, or other supported payment methods. You can also receive funds from other Swif users or external wallets.

4. How do I send money using Swif?

To send money, select the recipient from your contacts or input their wallet address, enter the amount, and confirm the transaction. You will be able to see a detailed summary before finalizing.

5. What is USDCXLM?

USDCXLM is the US Dollar Coin (USDC) tokenized on the Stellar blockchain. All transactions on Swif are converted to USDCXLM for seamless cross-border transfers.

6. What fees are associated with transactions?

Swif charges a small platform fee in addition to any network fees imposed by Stellar or payment gateways. Ramp fees depend on the service provider you use for funding or withdrawal.

7. How secure is my Swif wallet?

Your Swif wallet uses industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data. We also implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) to ensure your account’s safety.

8. Can I use Swif internationally?

Yes, Swif is designed for cross-border payments, and you can send and receive money across different countries with minimal fees and swift transactions.

9. Does Swif support multiple currencies?

Currently, Swif supports USDCXLM, and we plan to integrate additional currencies in the future. We are focused on providing seamless cross-border transactions using the Stellar network.

10. How do I withdraw funds from my Swif wallet?

You can withdraw funds by selecting your preferred withdrawal method. This could be through bank transfer, linked card, or supported withdrawal gateways.

11. What should I do if I have a problem with my transaction?

If you encounter any issues, you can contact Swif support through the app or email. Our support team is available 24/7 to help resolve any transaction-related problems.

12. What is liquidity management on Swif?

Liquidity management allows users to provide liquidity to the Swif platform and earn rewards based on their contributions. You can manage your liquidity settings directly from the app.

13. How do I buy and sell tokens on Swif?

You can buy and sell tokens using the Stellar DEX or a custom backend exchange integrated within the Swif platform. Simply select your token of choice and enter the amount you wish to buy or sell.

14. Is there a mobile app for Swif?

Yes, Swif has a mobile app available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.

15. How can I contact Swif support?

For support, you can visit the "Contact Us" section in the Swif app or email us at support@swif.com. We aim to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible.
