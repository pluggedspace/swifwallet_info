Our Mission

Swif is a cross-border international wallet designed to make remittance and global payments simple, cost-effective, and secure. Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses by providing seamless financial solutions that transcend borders.

Use Cases

  • Cross-Border Payments: Send money across borders quickly with low transaction fees.
  • Investment Opportunities: Manage your investments and access financial tools for growth.
  • Utility Payments: Pay for utilities like electricity, water, and internet conveniently.
  • IBAN Functionality: Obtain a virtual IBAN for international banking needs.
  • Shopping: Pooled shopping for cost savings and bulk purchases.

Development Stage

Swif is currently in the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) phase, focusing on building a robust wallet with basic functionalities:

  • Wallet creation and management
  • Integration with Stellar for token transactions
  • Onboarding and offboarding ramps for fiat-to-crypto conversions

Future development includes expanding DeFi capabilities, introducing liquidity management, and enabling cross-chain token swaps.

Token Development

Swif leverages USDC on the Stellar blockchain (USDCXLM) as the primary token for transactions. All transactions are converted into USDC to simplify currency exchange and provide stability. Future plans include:

  • Integration with Stellar-based DeFi protocols
  • Enabling users to buy/sell tokens directly via the Stellar DEX
  • Rewarding liquidity providers through incentive programs

Key Features

  • Low transaction fees for remittances
  • Multi-currency support
  • Highly secure transactions using blockchain technology
  • Prefilled user details for seamless onboarding

Why Choose Swif?

Swif stands out with its commitment to user-friendliness, robust security, and low-cost financial solutions. Whether you're an individual or a business, Swif is here to simplify your global financial operations.
